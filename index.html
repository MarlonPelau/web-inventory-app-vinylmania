why vinyl?

Within various music streaming platforms of today, there's no song or album that can compare to the sound-quality of vinyl. Here at 'vinylmania' most of our in-stock releases are delivered straight from the record pressing plant to us. We do occasionally have high-quality, secondhand vinyl of rare records that's no longer in the process of being reprinted. If quality of music and rare collectibles are your thing, get the vinyl.
